Guild_description

The Mercenaries and Adventurers Guild is a neutral organization located in several cities and fortresses across the country. Founded to maintain order among masterless warriors, it serves as a bridge between contractors and independent fighters—including ronin, shugenja, wandering swordsmen, yokai hunters, and military specialists.

Contract intermediation Links mercenaries to clients (civilians, nobles, temples, even daimyōs)
Mission management Organizes combat missions, escort, espionage, investigation, etc.
Member classification Evaluates, ranks and promotes groups based on performance and fame
Center of reputation and fame Groups gain renown, recognition, and custom orders
Recruitment and reintegration Accepts ronin, war orphans, veterans, or creatures under magical contract

Military and Political Neutrality

Although it provides warriors for wars, the guild maintains a neutral position between the clans.
Its code of conduct prohibits:
- Fighting for both sides simultaneously
- Guild groups from participating in internal political coups
- Acts against the guild headquarters or its members

Mercenaries can be hired for wars, but:
- The contract is registered and visible to the guild
- Conflicting missions are temporarily blocked
- The guild never directly participates in conflicts
“The guild does not care about the throne. Only about the coin.”
